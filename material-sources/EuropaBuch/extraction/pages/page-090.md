**[VISUAL: PROTECTION MEASURES MATCHING EXERCISE]**
A matching exercise where students connect protection measure descriptions to their names. Three protection types are shown:
- **Basisschutz** (Basic protection): Prevents direct contact with live parts through insulation, covers, or barriers
- **Fehlerschutz** (Fault protection): Prevents dangerous voltages from appearing on normally non-energized parts (like housings) by automatic disconnection
- **Zusatzschutz** (Additional protection): Typically achieved with RCDs (Fehlerstrom-Schutzschalter) as an extra layer of protection
