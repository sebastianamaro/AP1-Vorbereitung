A web contact form mockup at "http://www.TestKunde.de/Kontakt.html" containing several GDPR compliance issues for students to identify:
- Form fields: Name, Vorname, E-Mail, Telefon, Kommentar
- Pre-checked checkbox for newsletter subscription
- Pre-checked checkbox for consent to use contact data for advertising
- Submit button (Absenden)

GDPR issues to identify include:
1. No HTTPS (security concern)
2. Pre-checked consent boxes (violates opt-in requirement)
3. Bundled consents (newsletter + advertising should be separate)
4. Missing privacy policy link
5. Missing information about data processing purposes
