- Kriterien (Criteria): Leistungsfähigkeit, Wirtschaftlichkeit, Erweiterbarkeit, Zukunftsfähigkeit
- Gewichtung (Weight): 20%, 50%, 15%, 15%
- Columns for each option being compared (Desktop PC, Laptop, Tablet or similar)
- Points (0-10) and weighted scores
- Sum row for final comparison

Students must fill in scores for each criterion and calculate the weighted totals to determine the best option.
