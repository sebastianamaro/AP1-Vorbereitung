## Tips for Using the Simulations

### Before the Simulation

1. **Create Real Exam Conditions:**
   - Plan 90 minutes of time
   - Only use a calculator (non-programmable) as an aid
   - Undisturbed environment

2. **Prepare Materials:**
   - Pen and paper for network diagrams/charts
   - Calculator
   - Clock/Timer

### During the Simulation

1. **Time Management:**
   - Note points - more points = more time
   - Don't get stuck on one task
   - At least look at all tasks

2. **Solution Strategy:**
   - Read the task carefully
   - For calculations: Document your work
   - For explanations: Use structure/bullet points

### After the Simulation

1. **Self-Assessment:**
   - Compare solutions
   - Award points honestly
   - Identify weaknesses

2. **Follow-up Work:**
   - Analyze errors
   - Review corresponding chapters
   - Practice similar tasks

## Passing Threshold

- **Points achieved:** at least 50 out of 100 points
- **Recommendation:** Set a goal of at least 70 points to pass safely
- **When uncertain:** Complete all tasks, even if unsure - partial points are possible!
