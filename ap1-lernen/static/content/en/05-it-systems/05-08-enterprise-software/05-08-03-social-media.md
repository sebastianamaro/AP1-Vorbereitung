### Opportunities of Social Media

| Opportunity | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Reach** | Reach millions of potential customers |
| **Cost** | Inexpensive compared to traditional advertising |
| **Dialog** | Direct exchange with customers |
| **Targeting** | Precise audience targeting |
| **Speed** | Real-time communication |
| **Authenticity** | Personal insights into the company |
| **Feedback** | Quick feedback on products |

---

### Risks of Social Media

| Risk | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Shitstorm** | Wave of negative comments |
| **Reputation damage** | Thoughtless posts can harm |
| **Data protection** | Accidental publication of sensitive data |
| **Time investment** | Professional management requires resources |
| **Loss of control** | Content is shared and commented on |
| **Dependency** | Algorithm changes affect reach |

---

### Social Media Guidelines

**Social media guidelines** are policies for employees on handling social media.

| Regulation | Content |
|------------|---------|
| **Transparency** | Disclose company affiliation |
| **Confidentiality** | Don't share internal information |
| **Respect** | No negative statements about competitors |
| **Private vs. Professional** | Separation or labeling |
| **Copyright** | Only own or licensed content |
| **Responsibility** | Everyone is responsible for their posts |

---

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) in Social Media

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Reach** | How many people see the content |
| **Engagement** | Interactions (likes, comments, shares) |
| **Followers** | Number of subscribers |
| **Click-through rate (CTR)** | Clicks on links / impressions |
| **Conversion** | Purchases or sign-ups through social media |
| **Sentiment** | Tone of comments (positive/negative) |

## Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1
Name three social media platforms and their typical use in business.

**Solution:**
1. **LinkedIn:** B2B marketing, recruiting, professional networking
2. **Instagram:** Product presentation, employer branding, visual content
3. **YouTube:** Tutorials, product videos, training material

### Exercise 2
A company wants to use social media. Name two opportunities and two risks.

**Solution:**

**Opportunities:**
1. **Large reach:** Millions of potential customers can be reached
2. **Direct dialog:** Fast communication with customers possible

**Risks:**
1. **Shitstorm:** Negative comments can spread quickly
2. **Reputation damage:** Thoughtless posts can harm company image

### Exercise 3
What should be regulated in social media guidelines for employees?

**Solution:**
- **Transparency:** Employees should disclose their company affiliation
- **Confidentiality:** Internal information must not be shared
- **Respectful behavior:** No negative statements about competitors or colleagues
- **Copyright:** Only use own or licensed content
- **Responsibility:** Everyone is responsible for their own posts
