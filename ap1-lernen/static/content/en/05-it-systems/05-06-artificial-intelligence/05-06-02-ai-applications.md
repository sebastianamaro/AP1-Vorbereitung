## Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1
Name three examples of AI applications in everyday life.

**Solution:**
1. **Voice assistants:** Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant - understand speech and execute commands
2. **Recommendation systems:** Netflix, Spotify - suggest personalized content
3. **Navigation:** Google Maps - optimizes routes and predicts traffic

(Additional: Spam filters, face recognition, translators)

### Exercise 2
A company wants to use AI for customer service. Name two advantages and two limitations.

**Solution:**

**Advantages:**
1. **24/7 availability:** Customers can get help at any time
2. **Quick answers:** Immediate response to common questions

**Limitations:**
1. **Complex inquiries:** Chatbot cannot solve individual problems
2. **Lack of empathy:** Human contact is better for complaints

### Exercise 3
What is "AIOps" and what is it used for?

**Solution:** **AIOps (Artificial Intelligence for IT Operations)** refers to the use of AI in IT operations. Applications:
- Automatic monitoring and alerting
- Root cause analysis for incidents
- Capacity planning and forecasting
- Automatic error resolution

The goal is automation and improvement of IT operations through AI.
