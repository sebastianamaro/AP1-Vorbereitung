# IT Systems - Chapter Overview

## Introduction

The IT Systems chapter covers the technical foundations of information technology - from number systems to hardware components through to cloud computing and artificial intelligence. These topics form the foundation for understanding modern IT infrastructures.

---

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you should be able to:
- Master number systems and their conversion
- Know hardware components and their functions
- Understand operating systems and their tasks
- Explain virtualization and cloud computing
- Apply backup strategies
- Know the fundamentals of artificial intelligence [NEW 2025]
- Understand the EU AI Act [NEW 2025]
- Know enterprise software (ERP, CRM, SCM) [NEW 2025]
