# Business Environment

## Learning Objectives

After this section, you will be able to:
- Distinguish the three economic sectors
- Name stakeholders of a company
- Analyze the business environment
- Classify the role of IT companies in the economic system

### IT Companies in the Economic System

| Sector | IT Examples |
|--------|-------------|
| **Primary** | IT in agriculture (Smart Farming) |
| **Secondary** | Hardware manufacturers, chip production |
| **Tertiary** | System integrators, cloud providers, IT consulting |

Most IT companies belong to the **tertiary sector** (services).

### Stakeholders (Interest Groups)

**Stakeholders** are all persons and groups that have an interest in the company or are affected by its actions.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    STAKEHOLDERS                                      │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                                      │
│                         ┌───────────────┐                           │
│           Owners        │               │    Customers              │
│         (Shareholders)  │   COMPANY     │   (Buyers)                │
│                 ↘       │               │       ↙                   │
│                         │   ConSystem   │                           │
│    Employees ──────────►│     GmbH      │◄──────── Suppliers        │
│                         │               │                           │
│                 ↗       │               │       ↖                   │
│            Government   │               │    Banks                  │
│          (Authorities)  └───────────────┘  (Capital providers)      │
│                                 ↑                                    │
│                           Society                                    │
│                          (Public)                                    │
│                                                                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Stakeholder Interests

| Stakeholder | Interests |
|-------------|-----------|
| **Owners** | Profit, value increase, company success |
| **Employees** | Job security, good salary, development |
| **Customers** | Quality, fair prices, good service |
| **Suppliers** | Payment security, long-term relationship |
| **Banks** | Interest, repayment, creditworthiness |
| **Government** | Taxes, jobs, compliance with laws |
| **Society** | Environmental protection, social responsibility |

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| **Primary sector** | Raw material extraction (agriculture, mining) |
| **Secondary sector** | Manufacturing industry |
| **Tertiary sector** | Services |
| **Stakeholder** | All interest groups of a company |
| **Shareholder** | Equity holder/owner |
| **Macro environment** | External, non-influenceable factors |
| **Micro environment** | Close environment, partially influenceable |

## Exam Tips

1. **Sector classification:**
   - IT service provider = Tertiary (service)
   - Hardware manufacturer = Secondary (manufacturing)

2. **Remember stakeholders:**
   - **O**wners, **E**mployees, **C**ustomers, **S**uppliers, **B**anks, **G**overnment
   - Mnemonic: "**O**ne **E**mployee **C**alls **S**uppliers **B**efore **G**overnment"

3. **Typical exam questions:**
   - Which stakeholders does an IT company have?
   - Difference between shareholder/stakeholder?
   - Assignment to economic sectors

## Practice Exercises

### Exercise 1: Sector Classification

Assign the following companies to the correct sector:

a) A cloud service provider
b) A chip manufacturer (Intel, AMD)
c) A data center offering server hosting
d) A copper mine for cable production

<details>
<summary>Show Solution</summary>

a) **Tertiary sector** - Cloud is a service
b) **Secondary sector** - Production/manufacturing of hardware
c) **Tertiary sector** - Hosting is a service
d) **Primary sector** - Raw material extraction

</details>

### Exercise 2: Stakeholder Analysis

ConSystem GmbH is planning to introduce a new ERP system. Which stakeholders are affected and what are their interests?

<details>
<summary>Show Solution</summary>

| Stakeholder | Interest in ERP System |
|-------------|------------------------|
| **Management** | Increased efficiency, cost reduction, better data |
| **Employees** | User-friendliness, training, no extra work |
| **IT Department** | Maintainability, integration, support |
| **Customers** | Faster order processing |
| **Suppliers** | Better ordering processes |
| **Banks** | ROI of the investment |

</details>

### Exercise 3: Case Study

A new data protection law (GDPR amendment) is passed. To which part of the business environment does this belong and why can the company not directly influence it?

<details>
<summary>Show Solution</summary>

**Classification:** Macro environment (Politics/Law)

**Justification:**
- Laws are passed by government bodies
- Individual companies cannot directly influence laws
- The company must adapt to the law (compliance)

**Possible indirect influence:**
- Through industry associations (e.g., BITKOM)
- Lobbying
- Comments on legislative proposals

</details>
