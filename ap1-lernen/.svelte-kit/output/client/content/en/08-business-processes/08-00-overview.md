## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:

### Understand Enterprises
- Describe economic sectors and the business environment
- Distinguish market forms (monopoly, oligopoly, polypoly)
- Explain distribution channels for IT products
- Present and compare organizational structures

### Evaluate Economic Viability
- Distinguish and calculate cost types
- Calculate ROI and TCO for IT investments
- Conduct and evaluate utility analyses
- Systematically compare offers

### Conduct Procurement
- Describe the procurement process from demand determination to ordering
- Understand and optimize value chains

### Apply Contract Law
- Distinguish different contract types
- Name essential contract components
- Know rights in case of contract disruptions

### Calculate Prices
- Perform markup calculations
- Calculate IT hourly rates

### Implement Service Management
- Classify service requests
- Work with ticket systems
- Explain service levels
- Understand and apply SLAs

## Important Calculations in This Chapter

1. **Utility Analysis:** Weighted point evaluation for decision-making
2. **Purchase Price Calculation:** List price - Discount + Procurement costs
3. **ROI:** (Profit / Investment) × 100
4. **TCO:** All costs over the usage period
5. **Hourly Rate Calculation:** Personnel costs / productive hours
