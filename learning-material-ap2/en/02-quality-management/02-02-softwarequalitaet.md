# Software Quality (ISO/IEC 25000)

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should be able to:
- Know the purpose and origin of the ISO/IEC 25000 standard
- Name and explain the essential quality characteristics
- Distinguish functional from non-functional requirements

---

## Core Content

### ISO/IEC 25000 (SQuaRE)

**ISO/IEC 25000** (*Software Quality Requirements and Evaluation*, "SQuaRE") is the international standard for the **quality of software products**. It evolved from two older standards:

- **ISO/IEC 9126** – defined a **quality model** for evaluating software products
- **ISO/IEC 14598** – defined the **process** of software product evaluation

> *"ISO/IEC 25000 is the result of the evolution of several other standards; specifically from ISO/IEC 9126 (quality model) and ISO/IEC 14598 (evaluation process)."*

### Quality characteristics (according to ISO/IEC 25010)

| Characteristic | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|
| **Functional suitability** | Does the software perform the required functions correctly? |
| **Reliability** | Availability, fault tolerance, recoverability |
| **Usability** | Learnability, operability, accessibility |
| **Efficiency (performance)** | Time behavior, resource consumption |
| **Compatibility** | Coexistence and interoperability with other systems |
| **Security** | Confidentiality, integrity, non-repudiation |
| **Maintainability** | Modularity, analyzability, modifiability, testability |
| **Portability** | Adaptability, installability, replaceability |

### Functional vs. non-functional

- **Functional requirements:** *what* the system should do (functions).
- **Non-functional requirements:** *how well* (performance, security, usability …) – often exactly the quality characteristics above.

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|---------|-----------|
| **ISO/IEC 25000 (SQuaRE)** | Family of standards for software quality and its evaluation |
| **ISO/IEC 9126** | Predecessor standard (quality model) |
| **Non-functional requirement** | Quality requirement (performance, security …) |

---

## Exam Tips

- The **origin** (9126 + 14598 → 25000) is a typical short question.
- Have a few **quality characteristics** ready, each with a short explanation.
- Reliably distinguish **functional vs. non-functional** – also appears in GA1 (requirements analysis).
- Be able to translate English standard texts (the book includes a translation task).
