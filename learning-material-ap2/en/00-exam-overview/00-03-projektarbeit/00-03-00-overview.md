# The Workplace Project Work

The betriebliche Projektarbeit (workplace project work) is the **most important single component of the AP2 (50 % of the overall grade)**. It comprises the complete **planning, execution and reflection of a real project** (max. **80 hours**) – as a subproject or a standalone project, with an internal client or an external customer.

The component consists of four steps:

1. **Projektantrag (project proposal)** – application and approval by the Prüfungsausschuss (examination board)
2. **Execution** – the actual project work in the company
3. **Projektdokumentation (project documentation)** – process-oriented report (approx. 15 pages)
4. **Presentation & Fachgespräch (technical discussion)** – before the IHK examination board (max. 30 min.)
