# Presentation & Fachgespräch (Technical Discussion)

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should:
- Know the procedure and time frame of the presentation and Fachgespräch (technical discussion)
- Know what the Prüfungsausschuss (examination board) pays attention to
- Avoid the most common mistakes

---

## Core Content

After the proposal, execution and documentation comes the conclusion: the **presentation** of the project work before the **IHK examination board**, immediately followed by the **Fachgespräch**. Both together last **a maximum of 30 minutes** (guideline value for the presentation: ~15 minutes).

### Presentation

- **Free talk** on the project along the outline **Planning – Execution – Reflection**
- Slides **legible and not overloaded**; images/diagrams as a starting point for speaking freely (**no reading aloud**)
- **Keep to the time limit** (practice the presentation beforehand)

### Fachgespräch

- Subsequent discussion with the examination board
- Central content: the **background of the project work** – technical decisions, alternatives, in-depth exploration
- Prepare by thoroughly understanding the **technical background** of your project; the board often begins with a question about the project background and then goes into depth

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| **Fachgespräch (technical discussion)** | Examination discussion following the presentation, deepens the project background |
| **Prüfungsausschuss (examination board)** | Evaluating IHK panel |

---

## Exam Tips

Common mistakes (from preparation sources, "11 most common mistakes"):

- **Time exceeded** or significantly undershot → practice the presentation beforehand with a stopwatch
- **Slides overloaded** / text read aloud instead of spoken freely
- Presentation deviates strongly from the **documentation**
- Questions in the Fachgespräch not answered to the point
- Appropriate **clothing** and demeanor not observed
- Outline not oriented towards **planning/execution/reflection**

**In the Fachgespräch:** Try to answer every question – even an attempt earns points. If you don't understand a question, politely ask for it to be rephrased.
