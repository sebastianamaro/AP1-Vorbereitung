# The Three Written Exams (GA1, GA2, WISO)

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should:
- Know the content, duration and format of the three written exams
- Know which competencies are tested in GA1 and GA2

---

## Core Content

Part 2 contains **three written exams**. Two are job-related (GA1, GA2), one is cross-occupational (WISO). The job-related tasks are **holistic**: they are not about mere recall of knowledge, but about the **solution of complex, operational action situations**.

| Exam | Topic | Duration | Points | Tasks | Share |
|------|-------|----------|--------|-------|-------|
| **GA1** | Planning a software product | 90 min. | 100 | 4 | 10 % |
| **GA2** | Development and implementation of algorithms | 90 min. | 100 | 4 | 10 % |
| **WISO** | Economics and Social Studies | 60 min. | 100 | approx. 30 | 10 % |

### GA1 – Planning a software product

- Select and use development environments and libraries
- Define program specifications appropriately for the application (requirements analysis)
- Design user interfaces in a functional and ergonomic way
- Plan and carry out quality control measures

### GA2 – Development and implementation of algorithms

- Interpret program code and create a solution in a programming language
- Translate algorithms into programming logic and **represent them graphically** (UML!)
- Select test scenarios and generate test data
- Create queries for obtaining and manipulating data (SQL)

### WISO – Economics and Social Studies

Action-oriented questions, usually formulated as **multiple choice**, covering, among others:
- General economic/social contexts of the professional and working world
- Vocational training as well as labor and collective bargaining law
- Structure and organization of the training company
- Occupational safety and health protection, environmental protection
- Networked collaboration using digital media

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| **Holistic task** | Task along a complex operational action situation, not mere recall |
| **GA1 / GA2** | Bound/job-related written exams 1 and 2 |
| **Action-oriented** | Questions geared towards real action situations (especially WISO) |

---

## Exam Tips

- **GA2 is heavily UML- and algorithm-focused** – "representing algorithms graphically" often means in practice **drawing diagrams** (see the Software/UML chapter). Diagrams are rewarded well.
- **SQL** is relevant in GA2 ("queries for obtaining and manipulating data") – it belongs to the AP2 material (removed from AP1, see [Catalog Changes](./00-02-catalog-changes.md)).
- WISO: train with multiple-choice exercises and old exams.
