# Structure of the AP2 (Application Development)

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should:
- Know the four components of the AP2 and their weighting
- Be able to place the timeline (summer/winter exam) in context
- Know how AP1 and AP2 contribute to the overall grade

---

## Core Content

### Context: the stretched final examination

Since the reorganization of the IT professions (August 2020) there is no longer an interim examination, but instead a **gestreckte Abschlussprüfung** (stretched final examination) in two parts:

| Part | Timing | Weighting in the overall grade |
|------|--------|--------------------------------|
| **Part 1 (AP1)** | 18 months after the start of training | 20 % |
| **Part 2 (AP2)** | at the end of the training | **80 %** |

AP1 ("Setting up an IT-supported workstation", 90 min.) is the same for all IT professions. **This book covers AP2 for application development.**

### The four components of the AP2

| Component | Duration | Weighting (overall grade) |
|-----------|----------|---------------------------|
| **Betriebliche Projektarbeit (workplace project work)** (documentation + presentation + Fachgespräch) | max. 80 hrs project, max. 30 min. presentation/Fachgespräch | **50 %** |
| **Job-related written exam 1 (GA1)** – *Planning a software product* | 90 min. | 10 % |
| **Job-related written exam 2 (GA2)** – *Development and implementation of algorithms* | 90 min. | 10 % |
| **Economics and Social Studies (WISO)** | 60 min. | 10 % |

Within the project work, the weighting is distributed across **documentation** and **presentation + Fachgespräch** (together making up the 50 %).

### Timeline

| | Summer exam | Winter exam |
|--|-------------|-------------|
| **Submit Projektantrag (project proposal)** | January | August/September |
| **Presentation & Fachgespräch** | June/July (depending on the summer holidays) | mid-January |
| **Written exams** | early May | late November |

> **Note:** Only the dates of the **written** exams are set nationwide. All others are guideline values and can vary depending on the IHK (Industrie- und Handelskammer, Chamber of Industry and Commerce).

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| **Gestreckte Abschlussprüfung** | Splitting of the final examination into two chronologically separated parts (AP1, AP2) |
| **GA1 / GA2** | The two job-related written exams (90 min. each, 10 % each) |
| **WISO** | Economics and Social Studies (Wirtschafts- und Sozialkunde) (60 min., 10 %) |
| **Betriebliche Projektarbeit** | Real project with documentation, presentation and Fachgespräch (50 %) |

---

## Exam Tips

- The **Projektarbeit (project work) (50 %)** is the biggest lever – careful preparation pays off most here.
- The three written exams (GA1, GA2, WISO) together count for **30 %** – don't underestimate them, but the project work carries more weight.
- Plan backwards from the **submission deadline of the Projektantrag** (January or August/September) – it is the first hard deadline.
