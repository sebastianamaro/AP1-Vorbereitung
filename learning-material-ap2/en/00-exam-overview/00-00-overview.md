# Exam Overview – Final Examination Part 2 (AE)

This chapter gives you an overview of **Final Examination Part 2 (AP2)** (Abschlussprüfung Teil 2, second part of the final exam) for the IT specialist in **application development** (Fachinformatiker/-in Anwendungsentwicklung): structure, weighting, dates, the workplace project work, the written exams and the passing rules.

## The Most Important Point in One Sentence

The AP2 accounts for **80 % of the overall grade** – and the **betriebliche Projektarbeit (workplace project work) alone counts for 50 %**. Whoever plans well here and documents cleanly has the greatest leverage for a good final grade.
