# Passing & Study Strategy

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should:
- Know the Bestehensregelung (passing rules) of the AP2
- Know when a mündliche Ergänzungsprüfung (oral supplementary exam) is possible
- Have an effective preparation strategy

---

## Core Content

### Passing rules (Bestehensregelung)

The exam is passed if **all** of the following conditions are met:

- In the **overall result** (Part 1 + Part 2) at least **"sufficient"**
- In the **result of Part 2** at least "sufficient"
- In at least **three exam areas** of Part 2 at least "sufficient"
- **No** exam area of Part 2 with "insufficient"

### Mündliche Ergänzungsprüfung (oral supplementary exam)

In one of the written areas, given a corresponding result, a **15-minute mündliche Ergänzungsprüfung (oral supplementary exam)** can be requested. The result is weighted in a ratio of **2:1 (written:oral)**. It can be the deciding factor for passing.

### Study strategy

- **Biggest lever: the Projektarbeit (project work) (50 %).** Start early with the proposal and project choice.
- **Practice with old exams** to train task formats and time management – but check every old task against the [Catalog Changes](./00-02-catalog-changes.md) (moved/removed topics).
- **Actively practice UML and algorithms** (draw/code them yourself), since GA2 is diagram-heavy and diagrams are rewarded well.
- Use **error checklists** for proposal/documentation/presentation (see the project work chapters).

### Time management in the written exam

1. At the start (approx. 6–8 min.) **read all tasks** and determine the order
2. Prioritize by **achievable points**, not by preference
3. **Write something for all subtasks** – even attempts earn partial points
4. If the task is unclear: briefly **describe your own interpretation** and explain your solution approach
5. Work **efficiently** – no perfectionism, focus on content

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| **Prüfungsbereich (exam area)** | Graded partial exam of Part 2 (project work, GA1, GA2, WISO) |
| **Mündliche Ergänzungsprüfung (oral supplementary exam)** | 15-minute additional exam, weighted 2:1 with the written result |
| **Sperrfach-Regel (blocking-subject rule)** | No area may be "insufficient" |

---

## Exam Tips

- Calculate early which grade you need for which overall result – the project work carries the greatest weight.
- Use the book's **exam simulations** (Chapter 10) as a dress rehearsal under timed conditions.
