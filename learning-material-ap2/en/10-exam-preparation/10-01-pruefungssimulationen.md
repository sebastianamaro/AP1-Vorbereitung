# Exam Simulations

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter, you should be able to:
- Assign the six Prüfungssimulationen (exam simulations) of the book to the exam areas
- Know which topics are tested per simulation
- Use the simulations effectively as a dress rehearsal

---

## Core Content

The book contains **six Prüfungssimulationen** (exam simulations, Ch. 3) with complete **solutions** – two per written exam area:

| Simulation | Area | Focus topics |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **3.1 / 3.2** | **GA1 – Planning a software product** | Requirements analysis, functional/non-functional, development environment, GUI/ergonomics planning, quality assurance |
| **3.3 / 3.4** | **GA2 – Development and implementation of algorithms** | UML, OOP/polymorphism, algorithms/pseudocode, desk check, SQL/databases |
| **3.5 / 3.6** | **WISO – Wirtschafts- und Sozialkunde (economics and social studies)** | Law, economic sectors/legal forms, labor/collective bargaining law, sustainability (mostly multiple choice) |

### How to use the simulations correctly

1. Work on them **under real conditions**: stop the time (GA1/GA2 90 min. each, WISO 60 min.), no aids except permitted ones.
2. **Solve them yourself first**, then compare with the model solution.
3. **Analyze mistakes:** which topic (chapter) do I need to review?
4. **Supplement with past papers** – but with an eye on the [catalog changes](../00-exam-overview/00-02-catalog-changes.md) (shifted/removed topics).
5. **GA2 is diagram-heavy:** actively draw UML diagrams, check algorithms via desk check (Schreibtischtest).

| Term | Explanation |
|---------|-----------|
| **GA1** | Planning a software product (90 min.) |
| **GA2** | Development and implementation of algorithms (90 min.) |
| **WISO** | Wirtschafts- und Sozialkunde – economics and social studies (60 min.) |
| **Generalprobe (dress rehearsal)** | Simulation under real exam conditions |

---

## Exam Tips

- **Practice time management** (see [00-05](../00-exam-overview/00-05-bestehen-und-strategie.md)): first read all tasks, then prioritize by points.
- **GA2** rewards **diagrams** – be able to draw them confidently.
- After each simulation, specifically work through the **weak topics** from the subject chapters.

---

## Practice Exercise

**Task:** Work on a GA2 simulation (3.3 or 3.4) under 90-minute conditions, compare with the model solution, and note the chapters you need to review.
