# Study Plan & Strategy

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter, you should be able to:
- Structure your preparation for the four exam components
- Assign the subject chapters to the exam areas
- Set priorities according to weighting

---

## Core Content

### Priorities by weighting

| Component | Weight | Preparation |
|------------|---------|--------------|
| **Project work** | 50 % | biggest lever – early Projektantrag (project proposal)/project, clean documentation & presentation |
| **GA1 + GA2** | 10 % each | subject chapters + simulations |
| **WISO** | 10 % | practice multiple choice |

### Recommended sequence

1. **Early:** project idea + Projektantrag (project proposal) (deadline January or Aug/Sep).
2. **Ongoing:** work through the subject chapters, **actively practice** diagrams/subnetting/SQL.
3. **Mid:** work on the exam simulations under time pressure, work on weaknesses.
4. **Late:** past papers (with catalog comparison), WISO multiple choice, rehearse the presentation.

### Practical tips (from preparation sources)

- Follow the error checklists for **Projektantrag/documentation/presentation** (Ch. 00-03).
- **Diagrams are well rewarded** – draw UML actively.
- Check old exams against the [catalog changes](../00-exam-overview/00-02-catalog-changes.md).

## Exam Tips

- Invest time **according to weighting** – the project work first.
- Practice calculation/drawing topics (subnetting, network diagram, UML, SQL) **actively** rather than just by reading.
