# Your Own Role in the Training Company

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should be able to:
- Know the dual training system (duale Ausbildung) and its actors
- Classify the basics of labour and collective bargaining law
- Understand co-determination in the company

---

## Core Content

### Dual training (duale Ausbildung)

Training at **two learning venues**: the **company** (practice) + the **vocational school** (Berufsschule, theory). Legal basis: the **Berufsbildungsgesetz (BBiG** – Vocational Training Act). The **Ausbildungsvertrag** (training contract) governs rights/duties; the competent body is the **IHK** (Chamber of Industry and Commerce).

- **Duties of the trainee (Auszubildende):** duty to learn, diligence, following instructions, keeping the report book (Berichtsheft).
- **Duties of the company:** impart training content, release the trainee for school/exams, pay remuneration, provide training materials.

### Labour law (selection)

- **Employment contract** (Arbeitsvertrag), protection against dismissal, the **Arbeitszeitgesetz** (Working Hours Act), the **Bundesurlaubsgesetz** (Federal Leave Act)
- **Jugendarbeitsschutzgesetz (JArbSchG** – Youth Employment Protection Act) for minors
- **Probationary period** (Probezeit) in training: 1–4 months

### Co-determination & collective bargaining law

| Body/Term | Role |
|---------------|-------|
| **Betriebsrat** (works council) | Representation of employees' interests (BetrVG) |
| **Jugend- und Auszubildendenvertretung (JAV** – youth and trainee representation) | represents trainees/young employees |
| **Gewerkschaft** (trade union) | represents employees beyond a single company |
| **Tarifvertrag** (collective bargaining agreement) | Agreement between a trade union and an employer(s' association) on wages/conditions |

- **Tarifautonomie** (collective bargaining autonomy): the bargaining parties negotiate collective agreements without state intervention.

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|---------|-----------|
| **BBiG** | Berufsbildungsgesetz (Vocational Training Act) |
| **Duale Ausbildung** (dual training) | Company + vocational school |
| **Betriebsrat / JAV** | Interest representation bodies in the company |
| **Tarifvertrag** | Collective regulation of wages/conditions |

---

## Exam Tips

- Be able to contrast the **duties of the trainee and of the company**.
- Distinguish the **Betriebsrat (works council, BetrVG) vs. the Gewerkschaft (trade union)/Tarifvertrag**.
- Dual training: learning venues + BBiG + IHK as the competent body.
