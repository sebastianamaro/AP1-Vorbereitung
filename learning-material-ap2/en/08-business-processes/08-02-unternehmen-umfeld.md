# The Company and its Environment

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should be able to:
- Know the three economic sectors with examples
- Distinguish the main legal forms of business (Rechtsformen)
- Classify division of labour and economic interdependencies

---

## Core Content

### Economic sectors

| Sector | Area | Example industries |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| **Primary** | Raw material extraction | Agriculture, mining |
| **Secondary** | Processing/production | Industry, crafts, construction |
| **Tertiary** | Services | IT services, trade, banking |

(A **quaternary sector** for information/knowledge is sometimes named – the IT industry lies in the tertiary/quaternary area.)

### Legal forms of business (Rechtsformen – selection)

| Legal form | Liability | Characteristic |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Einzelunternehmen** (sole proprietorship) | unlimited (private) | one person |
| **GbR / OHG** (partnerships) | unlimited (partners) | Personengesellschaft (partnership) |
| **GmbH** (limited liability company) | **limited** (share capital €25,000) | Kapitalgesellschaft (corporation), legal person |
| **AG** (stock corporation) | limited (share capital €50,000) | shares, Kapitalgesellschaft (corporation) |

> **Kapitalgesellschaften (corporations, GmbH/AG):** liability is limited to the company's assets. **Personengesellschaften (partnerships):** the partners are usually personally liable.

### Division of labour & interdependencies

- **Division of labour** (Arbeitsteilung) increases efficiency, but creates **dependence on suppliers** (e.g. supply shortages of IT components).
- **Economic interdependencies:** cooperations, corporate groups, horizontal/vertical integration, supply chains.

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|---------|-----------|
| **Wirtschaftssektoren** (economic sectors) | Primary (raw materials), secondary (production), tertiary (services) |
| **GmbH** | Corporation with limited liability |
| **Personen- vs. Kapitalgesellschaft** | personal vs. limited liability |
| **Arbeitsteilung** (division of labour) | Splitting tasks across specialised areas |

---

## Exam Tips

- Have the **three sectors** ready with two industries each – IT is **tertiary**.
- **Liability**: GmbH/AG limited, Einzelunternehmen/OHG unlimited.
- Division of labour ↔ dependence on suppliers (opportunities/risks).
