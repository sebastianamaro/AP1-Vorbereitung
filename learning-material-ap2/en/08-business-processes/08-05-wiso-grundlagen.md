# WISO Basics: Wirtschaftskreislauf, Economic Principle, Prokura

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should be able to:
- Describe the simple Wirtschaftskreislauf (economic cycle)
- Apply the economic principle (Maximalprinzip/Minimalprinzip)
- Know the Prokura (commercial power of representation) and its limits

---

## Core Content

### Simple Wirtschaftskreislauf (economic cycle)

A model with two actors – **households** and **companies** – and opposing flows:

```
        Arbeitskraft ───────────►
Haushalte                          Unternehmen
        ◄─────────── Lohn/Einkommen
        Konsumausgaben ──────────►
        ◄─────────── Güter/Dienstleistungen
```

The **extended** cycle adds the state, banks and foreign countries.

### Economic principle

Managing scarce resources – two forms:

| Principle | Idea |
|---------|------|
| **Maximalprinzip** (maximum principle) | achieve the **maximum** output with a **given input** |
| **Minimalprinzip** (minimum principle) | achieve a **given goal** with **minimum** input |

*Example:* Maximalprinzip – win as many customers as possible with a €1,000 advertising budget. Minimalprinzip – win 100 new customers with as little budget as possible.

### Prokura

The **Prokura** (commercial power of representation) is a **comprehensive authority to act** for the business, granted by the merchant and entered **in the Handelsregister** (commercial register).

- **Einzelprokura** (sole procuration) – the holder (Prokurist) acts alone.
- **Gesamtprokura** (joint procuration) – only **jointly** with another Prokurist/owner.
- **Limits:** no **sale/encumbrance of land** (without a special power of attorney), **no** granting of further Prokura, no "fundamental transactions" (Grundlagengeschäfte, e.g. selling the company, filing for insolvency).

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|---------|-----------|
| **Wirtschaftskreislauf** (economic cycle) | Flows between households and companies |
| **Maximal-/Minimalprinzip** | Max. output at fixed input / goal with min. input |
| **Prokura** | Comprehensive power of representation entered in the Handelsregister |
| **Einzel-/Gesamtprokura** | Authorised to act alone / only jointly |

---

## Exam Tips

- Distinguish **Maximalprinzip vs. Minimalprinzip** using an example (what is fixed?).
- Prokura: **Einzel vs. Gesamt** and the **limits** (no sale of land, no granting of Prokura).
- Wirtschaftskreislauf: name the flows (labour/wage, goods/consumption).

## Practice Exercise

**Task:** (1) Assign two situations to the Maximalprinzip or Minimalprinzip respectively. (2) May a sole Prokurist (Einzelprokurist) sell a company plot of land? Justify your answer.
