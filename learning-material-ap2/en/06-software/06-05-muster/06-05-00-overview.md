# Design Patterns (Entwurfsmuster)

**Design patterns (Entwurfsmuster)** are reusable solution templates for frequently occurring design problems – **not finished code, but proven architectural decisions**. In the updated catalog they are explicitly AP2-relevant as **architecture/design patterns**.

## The three categories

| Category | Purpose | Patterns (in this chapter) |
|-----------|-------|----------------------------|
| **Creational (Erzeugungsmuster)** | Encapsulate object creation | **Singleton**, **Factory** |
| **Structural (Strukturmuster)** | Compose classes/objects | **Facade** |
| **Behavioral (Verhaltensmuster)** | Interaction/communication | **Observer**, **MVC** (architecture pattern) |
