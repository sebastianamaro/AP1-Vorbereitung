# Data Protection – Fundamentals

## Learning Objectives

After this chapter you should be able to:
- Know the legal foundations of data protection (DSGVO – EU General Data Protection Regulation, BDSG – German Federal Data Protection Act) and the responsibilities of the federal government/states
- State the goal of data protection (Art. 1 DSGVO)
- Name the conditions for Auftragsverarbeitung (processing on behalf of a controller)

---

## Core Content

### Legal foundations

- **DSGVO** (Datenschutz-Grundverordnung, EU General Data Protection Regulation) – an EU-wide regulation that applies **directly** in all member states and takes precedence.
- **BDSG** (Bundesdatenschutzgesetz, German Federal Data Protection Act) – specifies/supplements the DSGVO at national level (opening clauses).
- **Responsibility:** The federal government regulates federal public bodies and non-public bodies (companies); the **states (Länder)** regulate their public bodies through **state data protection acts (Landesdatenschutzgesetze)** and have their own **state data protection officers (Landesdatenschutzbeauftragte)**.

### Goal of data protection (Art. 1 DSGVO)

> "This Regulation protects the **fundamental rights and freedoms** of natural persons and in particular their **right to the protection of personal data**."

Data protection therefore protects **people** (not "the data itself") from harm to their rights through the handling of their data.

### Auftragsverarbeitung – processing on behalf of a controller (Art. 28 DSGVO)

An external company may process personal data on behalf of a controller, but only under certain conditions, including:

- **Data processing agreement (AVV – Auftragsverarbeitungsvertrag)** in written or electronic form
- Processing **only on documented instructions** of the controller
- **Confidentiality** of the personnel involved ensured
- Appropriate **technical and organizational measures (TOM)**
- Support of the controller with Betroffenenrechte (data-subject rights); deletion/return after the end of the assignment
- Sub-processors only with authorization

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Explanation |
|---------|-----------|
| **DSGVO** | EU General Data Protection Regulation, directly applicable |
| **BDSG** | National act supplementing the DSGVO |
| **Auftragsverarbeitung** | Processing on behalf of a controller (with an AVV) |
| **TOM** | Technical and organizational measures |

---

## Exam Tips

- **DSGVO before BDSG** (EU law takes precedence; the BDSG specifies further).
- Art. 1 – protection of **people/fundamental rights**, not the data itself.
- Auftragsverarbeitung: be able to name at least **AVV, binding instructions, confidentiality, TOM**.
